Traditional roman spaghetti carbonara

Back to Homepage

Ingredients


  • 1 egg per person + 1 extra/li>
  • 50g of guanciale per person
  • 1pkg of pasta of choice
  • 50g of peccorino cheese per person
  • salt
  • pepper

Description

Cut the guanciale into cubes and fry them slowly in a large pan without oil. In a separete pot cook the pasta for 3 minutes less than the required time written on the package.
Break the eggs into a large bowl add freshly ground pepper and mix it. Grind the peccorino cheese and add it to the egg mixture and whisk it until the cheese is mostly dissolved.
Once the pasta is cooked for the aformentioned time take a cup of the pasta water and add a third of it to the egg-cheese mixture and whisk it thoroughly.
Add the pasta to the pan of guanciale once the guanciale is crispy brown. Add a bit of the pasta water and on low heat add the egg-cheese mixture and stirring and mixing the pasta
until the sauce is well spread and creamy. If necessary add more pasta water. Enjoy!
