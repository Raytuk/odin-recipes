The traditional Sauce of the Toscano family

Back to Homepage


Ingredients


  • olive oil
  • 5 to 6 big tomatos
  • 1 beef or vegetable boullion
  • 1pkg of pasta of choice
  • 500ml of tomato sauce
  • tomato extract
  • half a cup of red wine
  • 1 large onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • oregano
  • parsley
  • basil
  • parmesan chees
  • salt
  • pepper

Description

Cut the tomatos and onion into cubes. On middle heat cook the onion in olive oil in large pot and add a crushed garlic clove once the onions are glassy.
Add the tomatos and the wine and let it simmer for 2 minutes. Afterwards add the tomato sauce, tomato extract, parsley, basil, oregano and the boullion and let it cook for about 15 minutes.
During this time you can cook the pasta or your choice. To finish the sauce you season it with salt and pepper and serve it with a fresh sprinkle of parmesan.
Enjoy!
